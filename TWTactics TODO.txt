Tips & Hints:
-------------
Use Control to increase the effect of other keys (movement arrows, ...)

Use middle mouse button to switch between default and polygon or attack mode.

Use Alt + D to switch between TW village icon and rectangle shape displays

Use Home to return to your home

You can move the map by 
- left clicking somewhere on the minimap
- the arrow keys (hold control to move more)
- dragging the map
- right click and selecting one of the Center/Pinpoint options
- mousewheel or +/- to increase/decrease zoom level

The Delete key works for Polygons and Attack Plans

Use L to (un)lock the pinned village

Polygon drawing: Hold control for fixed horizontal drawing. Shift for fixed vertical drawing.
